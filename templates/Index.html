About Smart Travel

Smart Travel was founded in 2023 with a mission to make travel planning easier, more personalized, and more enjoyable. Our AI-powered assistant helps travelers discover new destinations, find the best deals, and create itineraries that match their preferences.

Our team consists of travel enthusiasts, AI experts, and customer experience specialists who work together to provide you with the best travel planning experience possible.
